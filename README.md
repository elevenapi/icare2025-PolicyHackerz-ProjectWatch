**PRO-FIL: P**ublic **R**eview and **O**pen-Contracting System for **I**nfrastructures and **L**ocal **P**rocurements

[cite_start]This project, developed by Policy Hackerz [cite: 1, 14, 32][cite_start], proposes a policy solution to address the systemic failures and lack of accountability in public infrastructure procurement[cite: 165]. [cite_start]The core goal is to implement transparency, accountability, and public participation at all stages of public procurement and infrastructures [cite: 196-202]. [cite_start]The need for this is highlighted by events like "Tino's Floods" which exposed a P17.4B infra failure[cite: 7, 10].

## üõ†Ô∏è TechStack

The policy solution mandates specific technological components for effective governance:

* [cite_start]**Platform:** A centralized, online, and publicly accessible platform known as the **Open Contracting Portal** [cite: 244-245, 255-256].
* [cite_start]**Data Management:** The portal must provide procurement and contracting data updated in **real time**[cite: 245, 256].
* [cite_start]**Analytics/Algorithm:** Implementation of the **Contractor Risk Index (CRI)**[cite: 247], which calculates risk scores based on four factors:
    * [cite_start]Dominance or exposure [cite: 248]
    * [cite_start]Market concentration [cite: 248]
    * [cite_start]Budget consumption [cite: 248]
    * [cite_start]Delayed projects [cite: 248]
* [cite_start]**Reporting/Engagement:** The portal must allow citizens to report suspected irregularities, corruption, delays, or substandard work[cite: 246, 257].

## üí° Methodology

[cite_start]The PRO-FIL policy solution is built around a framework of **Prevention** and **Participation** [cite: 193-195]:

* [cite_start]**Policy Goal:** Upholding transparency, accountability, and public participation to prevent corruption and collusion, improve projects, and respond to community needs [cite: 196-208].
* [cite_start]**Intervention: Open Contracting Portal**[cite: 244].
* [cite_start]**Intervention: Contractor Risk Index (CRI)**[cite: 247].
* [cite_start]**Intervention: Pre-Award Engagement** [cite: 249][cite_start]: Mandatory consultation with community leaders, CSOs, or academic institutions [cite: 250][cite_start], including a **Citizen Observer Pool** and **Integrity Screening**[cite: 251].

This methodology is anchored on established governance principles:

* [cite_start]**Open Government Partnership Framework:** Utilizing principles of civic participation, transparency, and the use of technology [cite: 232-234].
* [cite_start]**Community-Based Monitoring Framework:** Integrating citizen monitoring at all stages of the procurement process to lessen oversight and misuse of funds [cite: 236-237].
